Frod is a 2d platforming and puzzle game in which you control a purple living ball with legs named Frod, who is capable of moving things through telepathy. Your goal will be to reach the end to find out why you have woken up, but for that you will have to overcome different challenges.
  • Run, jump and manipulate objects on the stage.
  • 51 collectibles.
  • 2 possible endings
